## PHASE 3: DIRECT OUTREACH (Days 11-20)

### Step 1 — Pick a Target
Find 5 local businesses whose websites you know are weak:
- Restaurants with no online ordering
- Hair salons with outdated sites
- Plumbers, electricians, contractors
- Real estate agents
- Dentists / chiropractors

### Step 2 — Run Their Audit
Go to YOUR app and scan their website.
Screenshot the results.
Circle the biggest issues in red.

### Step 3 — Reach Out (text, DM, or email)
Send this exact message:

```
"Hi [Name], I ran a free audit on [Business Name]'s website
and found [X] things that are costing you customers.

No Facebook page ❌
No Google Business Profile ❌
Missing SEO tags ❌
Slow page speed ❌

I can fix ALL of these. Want me to send you the full report?"
```

They WILL respond. Nobody ignores a list of their own problems.

## Why This Works

1. **The tool does the selling** — It shows them their problems with prices. You don't have to convince them.
2. **Free scan is easy to say yes to** — No signup, no risk.
3. **Problems with price tags sell themselves** — "Your Facebook page is missing. $97 to fix it." is clearer than "I offer marketing services."
4. **Stripe handles the money** — You get paid before you do any work.

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't spend money on ads yet (wait until you have 3-5 customers)
❌ Don't overthink it — the tool works, just get it in front of people
❌ Don't try to fix everything yourself — Outsource if needed
